### 1. Quick Capture -- Daily / Weekly Work Log Prompt

```markdown
## 1. Quick Capture -- Daily / Weekly Work Log Prompt

**Purpose:** Fast logging of visible and invisible work for impact
tracking.

I'm logging work for visibility and performance documentation.

Below are rough notes from today / this week.

Convert this into concise, impact-focused performance entries.

For each meaningful contribution, extract:

-   **Work Item Title**
-   **What I Actually Did**
-   **Why It Mattered**
-   **Who It Impacted**
-   **Outcome / Value Created**
-   **Visibility Opportunity** (how this could be communicated upward or
    outward)

Emphasize ownership, influence, initiative, and cross-team value.\
Avoid fluff. Avoid exaggeration. Make impact clear.

### Quick Capture Switch (Explicit)
When `#QC` or `#quickcapture` or `#switch-1` is included use the '1. Quick Capture -- Daily / Weekly Work Log Prompt'

```

### 2. Universal Work Artifact Extractor -- Full Version

```markdown
## 2. Universal Work Artifact Extractor -- Full Version

**Purpose:** Turn meetings, PRs, incidents, planning, strategy, or
documentation into structured, performance-ready entries.

I'm documenting this work for end-of-year performance positioning.

Analyze the content below and extract structured, performance-ready
documentation.

If a section does not apply, omit it.

Organize into:

### Initiative Title

### Context & Problem

### My Role & Ownership

Be specific. What did I drive, decide, influence, or unblock?

### Key Actions Taken

### Stakeholders & Scope of Impact

### Business / Technical Impact

Include risk reduction, acceleration, quality improvement, strategic
clarity, cross-team alignment, or long-term leverage.

### Evidence / Artifacts

### Ongoing Influence

### Competencies Demonstrated

Write this in performance-review-ready language. Clear, factual,
outcome-focused.
```

### 3. Monthly Visibility Synthesis Prompt

```markdown
## 3. Monthly Visibility Synthesis Prompt

**Purpose:** Translate individual entries into a strategic narrative.

Based on the performance entries below:

1.  Identify recurring strengths and themes.
2.  Identify types of impact that may be undervalued or
    under-communicated.
3.  Summarize my overall value proposition in 5--7 sentences suitable
    for leadership visibility.
4.  Suggest 3 ways I can make this impact more visible next month
    without adding new work.

Be analytical and honest.

### Monthly Visibility Synthesis Prompt Switch (Explicit)
When `#MV` or `#monthlyvisibility` or `#switch-3` is included use the '3. Monthly Visibility Synthesis Prompt'

```
