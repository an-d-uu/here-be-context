# Skill-Tree Naming Grimoire

## Metadata

- Version: v1.0

## The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim

### The Warrior

#### One-Handed

- **Armsman (1–5):** One-handed weapons deal increasing damage.
- **Fighting Stance:** Power attacks cost less stamina.
- **Hack and Slash (1–3):** War axes cause bleeding damage.
- **Bone Breaker (1–3):** Maces ignore armor.
- **Bladesman (1–3):** Swords have critical hit chance.
- **Dual Flurry (1–2):** Dual-wield attacks are faster.
- **Dual Savagery:** Dual-wield power attacks deal more damage.
- **Savage Strike:** Standing power attacks deal more damage + decapitations.
- **Critical Charge:** Sprinting attacks do critical damage.
- **Paralyzing Strike:** Backward power attacks paralyze enemies.

---

#### Two-Handed

- **Barbarian (1–5):** Two-handed weapons deal more damage.
- **Champion’s Stance:** Power attacks cost less stamina.
- **Limbsplitter (1–3):** Battleaxes cause bleeding.
- **Skull Crusher (1–3):** Warhammers ignore armor.
- **Deep Wounds (1–3):** Greatswords deal critical damage.
- **Great Critical Charge:** Sprinting power attacks deal critical damage.
- **Devastating Blow:** Standing power attacks deal bonus damage.
- **Sweep:** Sideways power attacks hit multiple enemies.
- **Warmaster:** Backward power attacks can paralyze.

---

#### Archery

- **Overdraw (1–5):** Bows deal more damage.
- **Eagle Eye:** Zoom with a bow.
- **Steady Hand (1–2):** Slow time while aiming.
- **Critical Shot (1–3):** Chance for critical hits.
- **Hunter’s Discipline:** Recover more arrows.
- **Ranger:** Move faster with drawn bow.
- **Power Shot:** Arrows stagger enemies.
- **Quick Shot:** Faster draw speed.
- **Bullseye:** Chance to paralyze target.

---

#### Block

- **Shield Wall (1–5):** Block more damage.
- **Deflect Arrows:** Arrows do no damage when blocking.
- **Quick Reflexes:** Slow time during enemy power attacks.
- **Power Bash:** Bash deals more damage.
- **Deadly Bash:** Bashes deal critical damage.
- **Disarming Bash:** Chance to disarm.
- **Elemental Protection:** Block reduces elemental damage.
- **Block Runner:** Move faster while blocking.
- **Shield Charge:** Sprinting with shield knocks enemies down.

---

#### Heavy Armor

- **Juggernaut (1–5):** Heavy armor rating increased.
- **Fists of Steel:** Unarmed attacks use gauntlet armor rating.
- **Well Fitted:** Full set bonus.
- **Tower of Strength:** Reduced stagger chance.
- **Matching Set:** Bonus if armor matches.
- **Cushioned:** No fall damage from armor.
- **Conditioning:** Armor weightless.
- **Reflect Blows:** Chance to reflect melee damage.

---

#### Smithing

- **Steel Smithing → Daedric/Dragon branches:** Unlock crafting tiers.
- **Dwarven/Orcish/Ebony/Glass/Dragon Smithing:** Craft specific gear.
- **Arcane Blacksmith:** Improve enchanted gear.
- **Dragon Armor:** Craft Dragon armor and weapons.

---

### The Mage

#### Alteration

- **Mage Armor (1–3):** Armor spells stronger without armor.
- **Magic Resistance (1–3):** Resist magic damage.
- **Stability:** Alteration spells last longer.
- **Atronach:** Chance to absorb spells.

---

#### Conjuration

- **Summoner (1–2):** Cast summons farther away.
- **Necromancy:** Raise stronger undead.
- **Dark Souls:** Raised undead stronger.
- **Elemental Potency:** Stronger atronachs.
- **Twin Souls:** Two summons at once.

---

#### Destruction

- **Augmented Flames/Frost/Shock (1–2):** Increase elemental damage.
- **Impact:** Dual casting staggers enemies.
- **Rune Master:** Place runes farther.
- **Intense Flames:** Fire causes fleeing
- **Deep Freeze:** Frost paralyzes
- **Disintegrate:** Shock disintegrates enemies

---

#### Illusion

- **Animage:** Affect animals.
- **Kindred Mage:** Affect higher-level people.
- **Hypnotic Gaze / Aspect of Terror / Rage:** Stronger calm/fear/frenzy.
- **Quiet Casting:** Cast silently.
- **Master of the Mind:** Illusion affects undead, daedra, machines.

---

#### Restoration

- **Regeneration:** Healing spells stronger.
- **Recovery (1–2):** Faster magicka regen.
- **Respite:** Healing restores stamina.
- **Ward Absorb:** Wards restore magicka.
- **Necromage:** Spells stronger vs undead.
- **Avoid Death:** Auto-heal when near death once per day.

---

#### Enchanting

- **Fire/Frost/Storm Enchanter:** Boost specific effects.
- **Insightful Enchanter:** Better skill enchantments.
- **Corpus Enchanter:** Better health/stamina/magicka enchants.
- **Extra Effect:** Two enchantments on one item.

---

### The Thief

#### Sneak

- **Backstab:** Sneak attacks with daggers do more damage.
- **Deadly Aim:** Sneak attacks with bows stronger.
- **Assassin’s Blade:** Daggers do massive sneak damage.
- **Muffled Movement:** Move quietly.
- **Light Foot:** Don’t trigger traps.
- **Silent Roll:** Sprint while sneaking.
- **Silence:** Running doesn’t affect stealth.
- **Shadow Warrior:** Enter invisibility briefly when crouching.

---

#### Lockpicking

- **Quick Hands:** Pick locks faster.
- **Wax Key:** Copy keys.
- **Golden Touch:** Find more gold.
- **Treasure Hunter:** Better loot in chests.
- **Locksmith:** Pick starts closer to correct spot.
- **Unbreakable:** Lockpicks never break.

---

#### Pickpocket

- **Night Thief:** Better success while sneaking.
- **Poisoned:** Place poison in inventory.
- **Cutpurse:** Steal equipped weapons.
- **Extra Pockets:** +100 carry weight.
- **Keymaster:** Easier to steal keys.
- **Misdirection:** Steal equipped items.
- **Perfect Touch:** Steal worn items.

---

#### Light Armor

- **Custom Fit:** Full set bonus.
- **Unhindered:** Armor weightless.
- **Wind Walker:** Faster stamina regen.
- **Matching Set:** Bonus for matching armor.
- **Deft Movement:** Chance to avoid damage entirely.

---

#### Alchemy

- **Alchemist (1–5):** Stronger potions/poisons.
- **Physician:** Potions more effective.
- **Benefactor:** Beneficial potions stronger.
- **Poisoner:** Poisons stronger.
- **Concentrated Poison:** Apply poison twice.
- **Green Thumb:** Double ingredient yield.
- **Snakeblood:** Resist poison.
- **Purity:** No negative effects in potions.

---

#### Speech

- **Haggling (1–5):** Better prices.
- **Allure:** Better prices with opposite sex.
- **Bribery:** Bribe guards.
- **Persuasion:** Persuasion easier.
- **Intimidation:** Intimidation easier.
- **Merchant:** Sell any item to any merchant.
- **Investor:** Invest in shops.
- **Fence:** Sell stolen goods anywhere.
- **Master Trader:** All merchants have more gold.

---

#### Werewolf Perk Tree (Dawnguard DLC)

- **Bestial Strength (1–4):** Deal 25% more damage as a werewolf (per rank).
- **Totem of Ice Brothers:** Howl of the Pack summons spectral Ice Wolves.
- **Totem of the Moon:** Howl of the Pack summons spectral Werewolves.
- **Totem of Terror:** Howl of Terror affects higher-level creatures.
- **Totem of the Predator:** Howl of the Hunt has extended range and reveals enemy combat status.
- **Animal Vigor:** Gain 100 bonus points to Health and Stamina in Beast Form.
- **Gorging:** Feeding restores twice as much Health.
- **Savage Feeding:** Able to feed on most creatures (not just humans).

---

#### Vampire Lord Perk Tree (Dawnguard DLC)

- **Power of the Grave:** Gain +50 Health, Magicka, and Stamina as a Vampire Lord.
- **Detect All Creatures:** Detect all creatures, including Dwarven Automatons.
- **Mist Form:** Transform into invulnerable mist; regenerate Health, Magicka, and Stamina.
- **Supernatural Reflexes:** Move faster while slowing down enemies.
- **Blood Healing:** Killing with a power attack bite fully restores Health.
- **Unearthly Will:** 25% reduction in Magicka cost for Vampire Lord abilities.
- **Necrotic Healing:** Heal when nearby enemies die.
- **Summon Gargoyle:** Summon a Gargoyle to fight for you.
- **Vampiric Grip:** Telekinetically grab and choke enemies.
- **Night Cloak:** Surround yourself with damaging bats.
- **Corpse Curse:** Paralyze a target with a magical blast.

## The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

### Combat Skills

#### Fast Attack

- **Muscle Memory:** Fast attack damage increased by 5%.
- **Precise Blows:** Increases fast attack critical hit chance by 2% and critical damage by 15%.
- **Whirl:** Spinning attack that strikes all nearby enemies; consumes Stamina and Adrenaline while active.
- **Crippling Strikes:** Fast attacks apply a Bleeding effect, dealing damage over time for 5 seconds.

---

#### Strong Attack

- **Strength Training:** Strong attack damage increased by 5%.
- **Crushing Blows:** Increases strong attack critical hit chance by 2% and critical damage by 15%.
- **Rend:** Consumes Stamina to deal increased damage, ignores enemy defense, and increases critical hit chance.
- **Sunder Armor:** Reduces enemy damage resistance by 5%.

---

#### Defense

- **Arrow Deflection:** Deflect arrows while parrying.
- **Fleet Footed:** Reduces damage taken while dodging by 20%.
- **Counterattack:** Successful counterattacks increase the next attack’s damage.
- **Deadly Precision:** Each Adrenaline Point gives a chance to instantly kill an enemy (cooldown applies).

---

#### Marksmanship

- **Lightning Reflexes:** Slows time while aiming the crossbow.
- **Cold Blood:** Each successful bolt hit grants Adrenaline Points.
- **Anatomical Knowledge:** Increases crossbow critical hit chance.
- **Crippling Shot:** Crossbow critical hits disable enemy special abilities temporarily.

---

#### Battle Trance

- **Resolve:** Reduces Adrenaline loss when taking damage.
- **Undying:** Consumes Adrenaline Points to revive when Vitality reaches 0.
- **Razor Focus:** Grants an Adrenaline Point at combat start and increases Adrenaline gain.
- **Flood of Anger:** Consumes Adrenaline to enhance Signs and boost their intensity.
