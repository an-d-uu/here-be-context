# Status Framing Lexicon

Lexicon for shaping how status updates are communicated to control perception, reduce unnecessary concern, and minimize follow-up questions.

## Metadata

- Tool: ChatGPT
- Version: v1.0

## Balanced Status Updates (Low-Panic, Low-Follow-Up)

- Instead of: My coworker put in their notice and I am scrambling to reassign work, verify responsibilities, and handle knowledge transfer

- Use: This week’s planned tasks have been moved to next week due to recent team adjustments that required a short-term reprioritization

- Context: Use when timelines shift due to internal changes and the goal is to provide enough context to prevent follow-up questions without introducing concern

- Notes: “recent team adjustments” signals a real cause without implying disruption; “short-term reprioritization” reinforces control and containment
